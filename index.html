Ella Englishby

"A new voice for the cello,"

About

Ella Englishby joins a new breed of cellists aiming to usher in the next era of cello performance while preserving the classical music of the past. Using her strong foundation of classical training, Ella continuously explores broad musical styles, pushes the boundaries of cello performance, and seeks out musical collaborators from all genres. She has been playing the cello for over ten years and has studied under several well known Irish and international conductors including Gearoid Grant, John Doyle and Greg Beardsell. She has performed in the Royal Albert Hall, Carnegie Hall, The RDS and The SSE Arena. She is also well versed in recording and playing alongside singer-songwriters. In the past, she has worked with the likes of Barry Jay Hughes, Jack McAuley, Ben Reel, Sean & Zoe Conway and many more.

Contact

Ella has been offering a wide range of services including accompaniment, musical arrangement, professional recording sessions, lessons and so much more for a number of years now.
